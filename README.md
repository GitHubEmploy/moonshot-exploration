## Philosophy

> "The best way to predict the future is to invent it." — Alan Kay

Each idea here is evaluated not by feasibility alone, but by the product of:
- **Impact × Probability of Success × Personal Passion**

The goal isn't to execute all of them. It's to find the one that matters enough to spend years on.
