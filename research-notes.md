## Quick Reference

### Frameworks

- **First Principles:** Break problem down to fundamental truths, build up from there
- **Second-Order Effects:** What happens next? And then?
- **Pre-Mortem:** Assume the project failed catastrophically — what went wrong?
- **Regret Minimization:** What will I regret not trying in 10 years?
- **RAT (Rapid Aptitude Test):** Can I get to 80% understanding in 2 hours?

### Questions to Ask

- If this succeeds, what does the world look like?
- What's the smallest experiment that disproves the core hypothesis?
- Who has already solved parts of this problem?
- What would this look like if it were easy?
- What does my intuition say vs the data say?
- Is this hard or is it novel? (Hard = known path, difficult execution; Novel = unknown path)

### Escaping Local Maxima

1. Write down all assumptions
2. Systematically challenge each one
3. Ask "what if the opposite were true?"
4. Seek out people who disagree
5. Reverse-engineer a competitor's future product
