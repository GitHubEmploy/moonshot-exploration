# Moonshot Project Ideas

> Big problems worth solving. Ideas that seem impossible until they aren't.

## 1. Solar-Powered Atmospheric Water Harvesting Grid

**Problem:** 2.2 billion people lack access to safe drinking water. Existing desalination is energy-intensive and centralized.

**Approach:** Deploy distributed, solar-powered atmospheric water generators using advanced MOF (Metal-Organic Framework) materials that capture water from ambient air even at low humidity (10-20%). Each unit produces 10-20L/day. Networked mesh provides real-time monitoring and predictive maintenance.

**Key Challenges:**
- MOF material cost and lifetime
- Energy efficiency at scale
- Mineralization and filtration for safety
- Distribution logistics in remote areas

**Moonshot Metric:** $0.001/L water cost at 5M+ units deployed, zero CO2 footprint.

## 2. Real-Time Universal Neural Translation

**Problem:** 7,000+ languages spoken globally. Real-time human-to-human communication across language barriers remains clumsy.

**Approach:** Wearable subvocal EMG sensor array that captures neural signals to the vocal cords before sound is produced. On-device LLM translates intent directly to target language audio via bone conduction. No speaking required. Works offline.

**Key Challenges:**
- Signal-to-noise ratio in EMG capture
- Mapping subvocalization across diverse neurology
- Latency under 200ms end-to-end
- Handling tonal languages (Mandarin, Thai, Yoruba)

**Moonshot Metric:** 99% semantic accuracy across 200+ languages, <100ms latency, device cost <$200.

## 3. Autonomous Reforestation at Planetary Scale

**Problem:** We lose ~10M hectares of forest annually. Current tree-planting efforts plant ~6B trees/year but need 1T+ to offset emissions.

**Approach:** Swarm of biodegradable drones that:
- Assess terrain via LiDAR + multispectral imaging
- Fire seed pods with mycorrhizal fungi + nutrients
- Self-destruct into soil after 6 months
- Use wind patterns and thermal currents for energy
- Coordinate via mesh network for coverage optimization

**Key Challenges:**
- Seed pod germination rates vs hand-planting
- Drone biodegradation timeline and byproducts
- Biodiversity: selecting species mix for each biome
- Regulatory airspace in 200+ countries

**Moonshot Metric:** 1B viable trees/year deployed at $0.01/tree, covering 10M hectares annually.

## 4. Personal Carbon Capture Device

**Problem:** Hard-to-abate emissions (aviation, agriculture, cement) need direct air capture, but current DAC costs $600-1000/ton and requires large facilities.

**Approach:** Desktop-sized electrochemical DAC unit using solid-oxide electrolysis. Captures ~1 ton CO2/year per unit directly from ambient air. CO2 converted to synthetic graphite (stable storage) or sold to industry. Consumer price point targets $99/unit.

**Key Challenges:**
- Sorbent regeneration energy requirements
- Selectivity in ambient CO2 capture (0.04%)
- Scalable manufacturing of solid-oxide cells
- Market for end-products (graphite, e-fuels)

**Moonshot Metric:** $50/ton capture cost at 1B units deployed, net-negative energy.

## 5. AI-Directed Personalized Cancer Immunotherapy

**Problem:** 10M cancer deaths/year. Immunotherapy works but only for ~20-30% of patients. Personalized approaches are too slow (6-12 weeks) and expensive ($100k+).

**Approach:** End-to-end automated platform:
1. **Biopsy → Sequencing:** Same-day tumor WGS + RNA-seq + proteomics
2. **Neoantigen Prediction:** Custom protein diffusion model predicts immunogenic neoepitopes in <1hr
3. **Vaccine Design:** AI designs mRNA vaccine encoding top 20 neoantigens + immune checkpoint inhibitor
4. **Manufacturing:** Automated mRNA synthesis using enzymatic process, 48-hour turnaround
5. **Delivery:** Lipid nanoparticle formulation targeting dendritic cells

**Key Challenges:**
- Neoantigen prediction accuracy (currently 50-60%)
- Manufacturing at patient-specific scale (mg not g)
- Regulatory pathway for personalized therapeutics
- Manufacturing cost reduction to <$10k/dose

**Moonshot Metric:** <72 hours from biopsy to treatment, >70% response rate across all cancer types, <$5k/course.

## 6. Fusion Micro-Reactor for Distributed Power

**Problem:** 770M people lack electricity. Grid extension is prohibitively expensive for remote areas. Renewables are intermittent.

**Approach:** 10MW modular aneutronic fusion reactor using p-B11 fuel (no neutron radiation). High-temperature superconducting magnets in compact tokamak configuration. Factory-manufactured, truck-shipped, plug-and-play. 5-year refuel cycle.

**Key Challenges:**
- p-B11 requires >10x higher temperature than D-T (~300M°C vs ~150M°C)
- Breakeven (Q>1) not yet demonstrated in any p-B11 system
- HTS magnet cost reduction needed (current ~$10M/magnet)
- Tritium handling (for bootstrap D-T startup phase)
- Licensing and public perception of "nuclear"

**Moonshot Metric:** 10MW output, $5M unit cost, <1c/kWh LCOE, 100k units by 2040.

## 7. Ocean Plastic Remediation via Self-Organizing Autonomous Systems

**Problem:** 14M tons of plastic enter oceans annually. 5 gyres contain ~100M tons of microplastics. Current cleanup is expensive and slow.

**Approach:** Bio-inspired autonomous "jellyfish" drones that:
- Harvest microplastics via electrostatic attraction
- Process plastics onboard via catalytic depolymerization into precursor oils
- Upload location and catch data via satellite
- Self-organize into swarms using stigmergy (environmental signaling)
- Beach themselves for collection when full
- Solar-powered, zero emissions

**Key Challenges:**
- Microplastic detection and capture efficiency
- Onboard depolymerization energy requirements
- Biofouling and marine environment durability
- Interaction with existing marine ecosystems
- International waters governance and liability

**Moonshot Metric:** 90% reduction in floating microplastics within 10 years, zero bycatch, $10/kg collection cost.

## 8. Synthetic Biological Carbon Sequestration

**Problem:** Natural carbon cycle is out of balance. Current CCS is expensive and slow. Enhanced weathering needs decades.

**Approach:** Engineered cyanobacteria that:
- Fix CO2 at 10x natural rate via optimized RuBisCo
- Secrete calcite (CaCO3) shells as metabolic byproduct
- Self-limit population via quorum sensing to prevent blooms
- Require only sunlight, seawater, and CO2
- Sink calcite to ocean floor as permanent sequestration

**Key Challenges:**
- Containment: preventing uncontrolled environmental release
- CRISPR-based kill switches (reliability at scale)
- Ecosystem impact of calcite sedimentation
- Regulatory and ethical frameworks for synthetic organisms
- Scalability to gigaton level (requires ~10M km²)

**Moonshot Metric:** 50 Gt CO2 sequestered annually by 2050, <$20/ton, fully reversible via inducible kill switch.
